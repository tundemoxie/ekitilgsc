ABOUT US

Ekiti State Local Government Service Commission was established in 1996 upon the creation of Ekiti State on 1st October 1996 from the defunct Ondo State. The Commission became a legal entity by an Act of Parliament in the year 2000 with a law establishing the Ekiti State Local Government Service Commission and Other Matters incidental thereto or connected therewith.

DEPARTMENTS

APPOINTMENT&POSTING

This department handles the recruitment of new staff as well as posting of officers to all Local Government Areas and LCDAs.

BUDGET, PLANNING, RESEARCH&STATISTICS

This department deals with data and information collation with particular regard to annual budget, annual personnel estimate, annual report of activities, personnel records, and analysis of data for policy formulation and implementation.

ENVIRONMENTAL HEALTH SERVICES

This is the department that supervises and coordinates environmental health matters in the local government areas for the purpose of ensuring effective and efficient implementation of the state government policies on environmental health matters.

ESTABLISHMENT MATTERS

This department deals with issues relating to career progression, staff discipline and preparation of Personnel Estimates.

FINANCE AND SUPPLY

This is the department that processes all payments and accounting transactions of the Commission. It also handles the supply of stationeries to all other departments via the store.

GENERAL ADMINISTRATION

This department implements the policies and oversees general administration of the commission. The information(media and publicity), legal, audit, and works units are all under this department.

PROCUREMENT

Procurement Department is all about acquisition of goods, works, and services. It is the department that regulates the procurement activities in all departments.

SPECIAL DUTIES

This department handles special issues that connect the Commission with the staff and communities in Ekiti State, including palace staff matters and marriage registration.

TRAINING AND MANPOWER DEVELOPMENT

This department is responsible for capacity building of staff of local governments through the organization of short and long term courses, seminars and workshops for the staff.

INDEPENDENT DEPARTMENTS

TRAINING SCHOOL

The school was established in May 1999 and had its first set of students admitted in year 2000. The school admits students for two programmes namely:

  • Certificate in Local Government Studies
  • Advanced certificate in Local Government Studies

    • At the close of the year 2020, the school was upgraded to a Diploma awarding institution affiliated to the Ekiti State University, Ado-Ekiti. Unlike individual departments in the Commission, the Training School is headed by an Executive Secretary.

    PTAD

    PTAD is an acronym for the Local Government Pension Transitional Arrangement Department. As the name implies, this department is saddled with the responsibility of handling all issues relating to retirement, pensions and gratuities of staff. The Accounting Officer is the Executive Secretary.

    LOANS BOARD

    The Local Government Loans Board receives loan applications from staff from all Local Government Areas and LCDAs. It also handles the disbursement of approved loans and ensures their recovery. Loan types are not limited to credit, asset financing and mortgages are also facilitated by this department. The Board is headed by an Executive Secretary.
